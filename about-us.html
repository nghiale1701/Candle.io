OUR STORY

We are Candleio Studio. Our dreamy scents are inspired by nature and travel, moments and memories. We make our candles in our workshop using sustainable soy wax and phthalate-free perfume oils infused with essential oils. We pride ourselves on our meticulous craftsmanship, ethical production, and accessible prices.

Our Missions

We found that amidst the chaotic world around us, making candles brought us closer together. This provided us the time to create something we are both passionate about while affording us the ability to involve our family in the labor of love. We are a non-traditional blended crew with four kids, three fur babies, a son-in-law, and a soon to be adopted grandson, Hudson. We are making this company a family affair.

One of our goals is to offer an array of generously scented candles and essential oil products that can be enjoyed for personal use or as gifts. We are passionate and dedicated to hand pouring a candle that is an affordable luxury. We have learned that life truly is all about the journey and we are moving toward something greater. We have dreams about where Candleio will go but realize that we can’t get there without pouring ourselves into every aspect of our candle making. We aim to light up our customers' lives with incredible customer service and a burning passion for what we do, every single day.

Why Candleio?

  • We are proud to offer premium soy candles, which is an eco-friendly, clean burning, renewable and biodegradable wax.
  • Our candles have just three ingredients, soy wax, cotton wicks, and premium fragrances and they are phthalate free, non-toxic and dye free.
  • We offer custom art on select candle sizes. You can create a unique or a personalized treasure for anyone on your gift list: any city or town.
  • We make our candles and wax melts in small batches that are poured and labeled by hand.
  • We will make you our priority. We will provide you with stellar customer service alongside our high quality candles and wax melts.
